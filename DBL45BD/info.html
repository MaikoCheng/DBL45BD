About the project

Hold to flip me!

This DBL HTI + Webtech project contains 2 visualisations to help users do research.

The Arc Diagram shows which employees of certain functions send the most emails to others.

The Adjacency Matrix shows the sentiment of the emails between certain employees with certain functions.

Why did we make it?

Hold to flip me!

We decided to do this project because visualizing big data is becoming more important and useful every day.

Also, we want to look into the relation between an employee's job function and their email sentiment at work.

We also had the chance to improve our teamwork and coding skills in the process.
