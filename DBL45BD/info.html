About the project

This DBL HTI + Webtech project contains 2 visualisations to help us do research.

The Arcdiagram shows us which people with certain functions send most emails to others.

The Adjacency Matrix shows us the sentiment of the emails between certain people with certain functions.

Why did we make it?

We decided to do this project because we read papers proving that work engagement of employees is directly correlated to their productivity.

Also, we want to research the correlation between an employee's wellbeing and their relations at work.

We also had the chance to improve our teamwork and coding skills in the process
